WE SHARE MUSIC TO

A Social Free Music Movement

BeatBlender is a social & free music community. Artists raise donations by allowing other artists to be creative with their work.

We hope you become part of our community and whatever you are working on ends up being great...

HOW IT WORKS

Copyright Owners
Donate Free Music

Artist that upload music to us allow other artists to use the material in a modified way for new artwork. Similiar to Creative Commons Licenses.

Music Users (Remixer)
Donate Streaming Money

Artist that create the new artwork instead agree that the incoming streaming money will be donated to selected charity projects via the BeatBlender Company.

Donate Music

With us you don't donate money. You donate music.

BeatBlender collects the streaming money of the songs being created from music that our community uploads and directs it to careful selected charity projects .

No monthly payments required to be part of our community. Its all about free music culture.

CONTRIBUTE FOR GOOD REASONS

Your Music can
Create Substantial Value

Our Community is sharing music for good and inspiration. After you created a new track that contains samples from BeatBlender you can release it to all major streaming services . BeatBlender collects the streaming revenues and forwards them to charity.

Be able to
Trace Your Influence

Stay in Control of your Copyrights. Whenever new artwork is released containing your sound it must be registered with BeatBlender. This means that copyright owners can see the resulting artwork that your sound has inspired.

DISTRIBUTE

Our community strives for an easy and uncompicated licensing process. After you registerd your track at BeatBlender you can start distributing your sound on all available streaming services
