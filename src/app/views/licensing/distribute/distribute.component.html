Split Streaming Income

BeatBlender's mission is to create a free-to-use music market by donating the streaming money from remixes that use music from our platform to non-profit projects that make a positive contribution to our society. Activate your license by for legal distribution by redirecting your streaming income to us. Therefore you must add BeatBlender as Collaborator and assign 100% to it. To distribute your music, we currently recommend you to use DistroKid, as this distributor offers automatic income splitting.

When we get notified by DistroKid that you added our distrokid email address as collaborator and assign 100% to it the license for your song becomes active.
